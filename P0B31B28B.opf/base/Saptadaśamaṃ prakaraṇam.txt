vihārairvihariṣyāvaḥ || tānyapi pañca bhikṣuśatānyetadavocan - vayamapyāyuṣmantau

saṃtrāsamāpadyāmahe | tena vayamaraṇyāyataneṣu dhyānasukhasaṃsparśavihārairvihariṣyāma
iti | nirmitakāvavocatām - tena hi āyuṣmantaḥ saṃgāsyāmo na vivadiṣyāmaḥ | avivādaparamo
